Novroz in Afghanistan

Afghans have been taking part in Nowruz new year celebrations,

which have coincided with the country’s centenary of independence.

Decked out in colourful dresses and carrying national flags and plastic trumpets,

revellers filled the usually traffic-clogged streets and famous Blue mosque in the northern city of Mazar-e-Sharif.

People from all over the country – as well as some neighbouring ones – have been joining in the fun to celebrate the year.
